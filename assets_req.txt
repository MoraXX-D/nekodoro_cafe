1️⃣2️⃣ ASSET CREATION PIPELINE
Step 1 – Generate with Gemini
Generate single frames with fixed resolution.
Step 2 – Clean in Piskel
Align frames
Remove background
Crop exactly
Step 3 – Export Sprite Sheet
Consistent frame size.
Step 4 – Optimize
Run through TinyPNG.
Step 5 – Move to /assets/sprites
